10-Step Korean Skincare Routine

The 10-Step isn't just a routine - it's a lifestyle inspired by Korea's love for healthy skin and supported by many years of scientific research.


It's not about owning countless products, but about choosing the right ones and using them in the correct sequence, to achieve a dewy, ageless looking skin.

  1. 1. Oil Cleanser

    Oil cleansers are a skincare game-changer due to their ability to effectively remove all types of makeup, sunscreen, and impurities without stripping the skin of its natural oils. They are formulated with gentle oils that work to dissolve the oils on your skin, including sebum, which can attract dirt and pollutants throughout the day.

  2. 2. Water based Cleanser

    Following oil cleansing, water-based cleansers are integral to Korean skincare routine because they use gentle formulas to remove remaining impurities, sweat, and residual oil without drying out the skin. This step ensures thorough cleansing and preps the skin for subsequent treatments.

  3. 3. Exfoliation

    Remove dead skin cells, revealing smoother, brighter skin by exfoliating. It can be achieved through physical scrubs or chemical exfoliants like AHAs and BHAs. Regular exfoliation promotes cell turnover, preventing clogged pores and enhancing product absorption.

  4. 4. Toner

    Toner in Korean skincare rebalances skin pH after cleansing, preps for better product absorption, and hydrates. It often contains botanical extracts to soothe and refine skin texture, promoting a clear complexion.

  5. 5. Essence

    This little known “layer” for Western consumers is a lightweight, hydrating formula packed with active ingredients like hyaluronic acid and antioxidants. It penetrates deep into the skin to nourish, brighten, and improve skin texture, enhancing overall complexion and preparing it for subsequent treatments.

  6. 6. Treatments

    By treatments we mean cosmetics which target specific concerns like acne, hyperpigmentation, or aging. They include serums, ampoules, and masks enriched with potent ingredients such as vitamin C, niacinamide, or peptides. Regular use addresses skin issues, promoting a healthier, more radiant complexion.

  7. 7. Sheet Mask

    Sheet masks are cloth masks soaked in nourishing serums. They provide intense hydration and deliver active ingredients like hyaluronic acid and vitamins directly to the skin. Used regularly, they boost hydration, brighten complexion, and address various skin concerns for a rejuvenated appearance.

  8. 8. Eye cream

    Target delicate under-eye area, combating dark circles, puffiness, and fine lines with eye cream. Formulated with gentle yet potent ingredients like peptides and caffeine, it hydrates, firms, and brightens the eye area for a refreshed look. Regular use promotes youthful, radiant eyes.

  9. 9. Moisturiser

    Moisturisers seal in hydration, balance oil production, and form a protective barrier. They usually have lightweight formulas, often infused with botanical extracts which replenish moisture without clogging pores. Used day and night, it keeps skin soft, supple, and nourished, enhancing overall complexion.

  10. 10. Sun Protection

    Arguably the most important step of all, applying SPF prevents sun damage, premature aging, and hyperpigmentation. Lightweight, non-greasy formulas with high UVA/UVB shields skin from harmful UV rays. Regular use safeguards skin health and maintains a youthful complexion.
